Instructions

Basics

You are playing the EA company in form of pacman. Your goal is to maximize profits by collecting as much money as possible from your customers.

Try to avoid being catched by your customers

Customers

Rebelling customers that complain to you and will stop you from collecting more money.

Every customers has its own strategy to chase you down.

Ivan

Ivan is FUT player and patient. When you steal 30 of his coins he is leaving the box to hunt you. His home is the bottom right corner.

Marc

Marc is an average customer totally disappointed by your company and wants his money back. His home is the bottom left corner.

Peter

Peter reports bugs and is chaising you from the start. But since bugfixing is costly: simply run from him. His home is the upper left corner.

Kurt

Kurt is a former pro-gamer and realizes late (at least when 2/3 of all coins are gone) that you banned him. Oh boy, that dude is pissed! His home is the bottom left corner.

Items

FIFA Coins

The goal of every level is to eat all FIFA coins of your custumer without getting catched. One coin results in 10 points.

Bonus

In every level there are 4 bonuses that are BIG MONEY for you. If you get those, you will get strong enough to bann the customers. You can see this indicated by the customers turning blue. One bonus results in 50 points.

Banning a customer results in 100 points. The soul of the customer will return to the box before starting to chase you again.

Customer moods

The customer have two different moods that change the way they act during the game.

Scatter mood

This is the default mood. When customers are in scatter mood, they will just go to their home corner and stay there.

Chase mood

After a certain time the customers change their mood and want to go chasing you. This is indicated through the walls turning red.

Controls

Use your arrow keys or [W,A,S,D] keys to navigate pacman.

To pause / resume the game press [SPACE] or [ESC] or just click into the game area.

Controls

Use swipe gestures to navigate pacman.

Alternatively use the Arrow Buttons underneath the game area to navigate pacman.

To pause / resume the game, touch the game area once.
